About Advocate Prashant Singh

Professional. Focused. Client-oriented representation in criminal matters.

Advocate Prashant Singh is a dedicated criminal lawyer practising at the High Court, Lucknow. With over 10 years of experience, he has handled a wide range of criminal matters including bail applications, trial matters, appeals, and revision petitions.
